CSS3 Transforms and Transitions on Motion Pictures with thumbnails

Back to portal

Applying CSS3 Transforms and Transitions to embellish the user experience on the variation with thumbnails of experiment to test AJAX load of binary resources as base64 encoded texts. Binary resources in this case are images to display as a frame animation of stop-motion pictures.
Note: Browsers load images asynchronously, and therefore the case in this example is not an actual need. Images have been chosen as the type of resources to load from AJAX as the stop motion animations allow to perceive the performance of the process. Other case easy to perceive would be loading sounds for i.e. a MIDI sequencer.
Runs faster in Firefox. Chrome frame rate not sustained with more than three motion animations. Try pausing one or two.
